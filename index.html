Relax, Explore, Enjoy

Nendaz is a beautiful valley in the Swiss Alps, our resort is the perfect escape from the mundane. Housing options include an Italian restaurant, and ski or bare on mountainside terraces. Other amenities consist of an upscale spa, hot tubs, a gym and a climbing wall. Breakfast is available.

Amenities and Options for Everyone

Escape from everyday life and spend a few warm and relaxing days or with us. Enjoy the refreshing mountain air. Observe the village and the surrounding mountains on foot or with our bikes. Visit our spa or ski runs during a winter visit, or take a guided hike during the warmer months. We offer something for everyone!

An Escape For Any Time of Year

Whether you prefer a snowy mountain escape or a peaceful summer getaway surrounded by verdant mountains and valleys, we have it all. You can hit the slopes, and hold a snowman in the winter, and you can head out for a hike, picnics, and swimming in the spring and summer. Escape the ordinary with us!
